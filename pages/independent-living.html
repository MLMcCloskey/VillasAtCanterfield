Independent Living

At The Villas at Canterfield, each new day brings the opportunity to meet new people, explore new passions, and indulge in what you’ve always wanted to do. You’ll be trading the uncertainties of living alone and the responsibilities of maintaining a home for services and amenities designed to make your life rich with opportunity. Living here is like living at a resort. From the moment you walk through the door, you’ll notice our attention to detail. Elegantly designed community areas with rich, traditional furnishings are the perfect place to gather with family and new friends.

In addition to a grand lifestyle, we offer you security and peace of mind in knowing you can comfortably age in place at The Villas because on-campus personal care and memory care, as well as rehabilitation services and home care, are offered. The Villas at Canterfield does more that provide a beautiful way to live. We encourage lifelong learning and wellness-oriented health care – the mind, body, and spirit. Our full-time Activities Director makes sure we’re offering a host of enriching cultural programs and entertainment that guarantee you have plenty of choices for personal growth and happiness. We accommodate your preferred lifestyle by offering one- and two-bedroom designs, all with either sunrooms or covered patios. And because you have better things to do, we keep everything scrubbed, trimmed, polished, swept, pruned, and raked. Not up for cooking? Our talented chef prepared daily creations that can be enjoyed in our spectacular restaurant-style dining room.
